Screamsheet: The Night City Beat

JUSTICE DELAYED, CORPORATE-STYLE: OMNIAGRI'S RED-CARPET FOR CHAOS

By: Jax "The Lens" Vane — Session 14 (nudged to us by our Alt-Mes)

Heywood is burning, and the REO Meatwagon crews are clocking record overtime scraping bodies off the pavement — but the real horror show isn't the riot. It's the boardroom.

Embedded with families outside the REO fortress, waiting for a quote on the medically detained, when the action shifted. A phalanx of NCPD cruisers rolled up responding to reports of an incursion at Omniagri tower. But instead of a tactical breach, they're getting a masterclass in corporate stalling. An Omniagri suit stepped out to head them off: a steady diet of corporate bylaws, private security jurisdiction clauses, and "active-threat mitigation protocols."

While the cops flip through their manuals trying to figure out if they have a warrant for a Level 4 Corporate Zone — a convoy of heavy-duty Maelstrom rigs pulled up at the service entrance. No sirens, no caution. Security opened the gates wide. Chrome-plated nightmares with glowing red optics, deployed like a surgical strike team.

The NCPD officers looked at the gates, looked at the suit, and then looked at their shoes.

"If you're inside that tower right now, you aren't waiting for the police. You're waiting for a slaughter. Omniagri isn't protecting their assets; they're inviting the wolves inside to finish the job."

Stay low, Night City. And if you see a chicken-bucket logo? Run the other way.

Pulze Report: Cyber-Cluck Bio-Horror

UPPER MARINA AVIAN OUTBREAK

Article posted by a rival media, sourced by Pulze — Month 1

Upper Marina — Just another Tuesday morning took a lethal turn as the latest victims of non-consensual, exotic body-sculpting hit the streets, resulting in multiple confirmed casualties among civilians and NCPD units.

These subjects — reportedly rendered into grotesque, near-avian forms — were discovered wandering on the banks of the Marina FloatHomes, their bodies wracked by painful, shuddering spasms described by eyewitnesses as "clucking." NCPD attempting standard detention found the subjects violently distressed. The confrontation escalated critically when one avian subject reportedly "laid an egg" — a device that quickly detonated.

"Which back-alley bio-surgeon engineered this new, self-destructing street weapon, and how many more ticking time-bombs are wandering the sprawl?"

Authorities advising extreme caution: prioritise neutralisers over detainment.

Pulze's Rumour Mill:

Maelstrom "punishment factories" are responsible. It fits their modus operandi — snatching random people off the street, subjecting them to horrific modifications, then releasing them. Just for shits and giggles.

Pulze Report: Red Chrome Legion

LOCAL INTEL — POPULI'S PUBLIC HUMILIATION

Pulze — Month 1

Word on the street regarding the Red Chrome Legion. Turns out Populi is all act and no action. The once film-star-turned-gang-leader attempted to bring his signature cinema violence to the streets with a NovaArms Shotgun — a bid to revive his failed acting career. Like his most recent movie "Dangerous Deeds 7", it was a flop.

Word is, a group of Edgerunners broke into his compound, stole his weapon, and left him so injured Trauma Team responded. They even left a taunt on repeat on the RCL private comms channel.

"Maybe this crew should get the movie deal?"

Pulze Report: Heywood Combustion Spike

BODY COUNT LOTTERY PREDICTION

Pulze — Month 1

Insiders are speculating that due to a spike in combustion deaths, Heywood is going to top the body count lottery for the near future. These flames burn hot, leaving only ash and scrap alloy. A spokesman for REO Meatwagon explained that profits are up since vacuum cleaners are now standard issue for their response services, as the need for body bags has decreased.

Month 1 — Community chatter

Goto Dengo is blowing up phones. Ever since word got out that the Trun Disintegrator was retrieved, he has not stopped messaging (via his Elflines avatar) about how to implement the next part of the mission. Each day, sometimes more, the pudgy patron offers ideas and suggestions. His 1000eb contract remains valid.

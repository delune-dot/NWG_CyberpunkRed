Session 08 — The Scarlett Lady (Part 1)

← Session 07 · Session Log · Home · Session 09 →

Location: Scarlett Lady, moored off the Pacific Playground
Key Contacts: One Shot Rodney, Three-Toed Joe
Gig: Scuttle the Scarlett Lady (Playland by the Sea — 2000 eb) ❌ FAILED

Background

The Scarlett Lady — a "Lifestyle" cruise liner — had been moored off the Pacific Playground since the shipping lanes to Alaska became stalked by sentient mines and Hunter-Killer aqua-drones during the 4th Corporate War. The Animals booster gang moved in and control the overcrowded vessel. Only the Yacht Club (VIP area) retains any luxury.

The Job: Playland by the Sea management (Night City's premiere tourist destination) wanted the Scarlett Lady scuttled. With the offshore casino gone, patrons would return to Playland. Simple: get in, sink it, survive, collect 2000 eb.

"Dead easy," says Rodney.

Prior to this mission: The Scarlett Lady had suffered some incident leaving her listing heavily to portside (related to an earlier attempted heist). The Animals were on high alert, sourcing parts for maritime repairs. The party had Three-Toed Joe's contact details for water transport from previous gigs.

Inside the Scarlett Lady

The crew disguised themselves as workers and gained access.

They installed a virus in the ship's systems to overcharge the desalination plants — making them suck in a ludicrous amount of Morro Bay water and pump it directly onto the engineering chamber floors.

With the virus planted, the race was on. A second netrunner on the ship began attempting to overwrite Null Pointer's virus. The crew knew from research that this second netrunner was physically located in the strong room of the casino (the Yacht Club area).

The crew fought through Animal security forces in the ship's lower sections, then executed the first installed netrunner.

Mission end of Part 1: Virus active, Maelstrom breach countdown begun, need to reach the casino strong room and neutralise the second netrunner before they save the ship.

Also: while there, the plan was to steal the amassed Cred-chips the Animals use for gang activities.

Note on Timing

This session links to the H-Town handover period. The closure of the San Morro Bridge had been bad for LCRA commerce — a lot of infrastructure now rerouting through New Westbrook and Upper Marina. Sum Ting had noted that LCRA goods were suffering unacceptable losses on the extended trade route (a Road Closure gig was also offered this period).

Key Characters Encountered

  • The Animals — booster gang. Running a gambling operation in the Yacht Club. Extremely hostile. High alert.
  • Three-Toed Joe — ferried the crew across the bay

Session 03

← Session 02 · Session Log · Home · Session 04 →

Location: Guangbo Tower (Level 30), Little China
Key Contacts: David Ling Ho, Goto Dengo, Darril, One Shot Rodney

What Happened

Back at Guangbo Tower, this time reaching Level 30 — where David Ling Ho, head of the Gold Dragons and owner of the building, caught the crew snooping. Rather than throw them out, he offered them a deal: recover the Brimstone Shotgun, which had just been stolen from under their noses by a crew dosed up on Phoenix.

Before they could act, the RCL agents stormed in on Phoenix and took the shotgun.

The Phoenix Drug Discovery

The crew first encountered Phoenix here — a neural disruptor that flips pain signals to pleasure.

Phoenix mechanics: Pain = pleasure, ecstasy. Negates injury modifiers. Halves mortal wound mods. Heat Stroke: 2 fire damage per activation until 1HP → DV15 fail = Deadly Burn (6 fire dmg per round, immolation). "Better to Burn Out than to Fade Away."

The Goto Dengo Side (Same Period)

Around this time, Goto Dengo — a corpulent Elflines collector based in New Macau, Little China — also made contact. He wanted the crew to rendezvous with him to hand over Elflines collectibles and discuss further lucrative deals in the niche collectible market. "4 weeks till rent."

Side Gigs Offered This Period

  • Darril's Dojo gig: Lift consumer-grade extruders from an RCL shipment. Easy job, 500eb. (Serial numbers supplied.)
  • One Shot Rodney gig: Hamper Maelstrom hyperviolence against independent bodega owners servicing Upper Marina floathomes. 1000eb. ("Discussion around One Shot Rodney centers on whether the One Shot is how long his operatives last or how good they are.")

Night Market — Mr Ling Po (Little China)

Mr Ling Po ran a local night market in Little China around this time. See Night Market for offerings.

Key Characters Encountered

  • David Ling Ho — Gold Dragons. Hired the crew to recover the shotgun.
  • Goto Dengo — Elflines collector. Began the collectibles arc.
  • Darril — Local fixer. First contact for the extruder gig.
  • One Shot Rodney — Shadowy fixer. First introduction.

Outcomes

  • Agreed to work for David Ling Ho to recover the Brimstone Shotgun → leads to Session 04
  • Phoenix drug noted as an encountered threat

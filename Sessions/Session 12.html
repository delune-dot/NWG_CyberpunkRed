Session 12 — Omniagri: Getting the Dirt

← Session 11 · Session Log · Home · Session 13 →

Location: Omniagri Night City Regional Suites, Upper Marina
Key Contacts: Mamma Jamma, Sum Ting
Gigs: Get Clucked! (The Roost, 1000 eb) · Road Closure (shady Corpo, 1000 eb) · LCRA Road Investigation (1000 eb + 1 HQ upgrade)

Background

The Roost is a cooperative farmer group, scratching a living growing crops and raising animals away from the patented viruses and nano-parasites released by corporations to ensure market monopoly.

Omniagri is aggressively pursuing "positional slippage" against these farmers to ensure their Synth Chicken Franchelettes have exclusivity in the Night City market. Mamma Rodriues (a farmer) was killed by Fallanx, a hired Maelstrom enforcer, who posted the "smoke house" execution onto scream feeds as a warning to holdouts.

Mamma Jamma — our contact in the Roost — offered 1000 eb to take part in this operation.

The Omniagri Building

Located in Upper Marina, nestled between the Del Cordando Overpass and the Eastern fringe of Coronado Bay. Brutalist concrete exterior transforming into a petrochemical refinery aesthetic at height. Adjacent to REO Meatwagon.

Key floors:
- HR / Recruitment: Level 5
- Distilling: Levels 17–19
- Management mainframe: Level 21
- Poultry Processing: Levels 25–27

Environment: Upper Marina is under full NCPD blanket surveillance. Legal threats, not physical ones. Omniagri staff and local AIs are uniformly sour and disgruntled.

The entry: REO Meatwagon was expecting a big night (riots in North Heywood and Santo Domingo over a Biotechnica executive who blew the medical vending budget on a Faberge coat). High AV and ground traffic around the area provided cover.

Harbor recon: Aquatic insertion was impossible — Omniagri's waste extruders discharge super-concentrated excrement into the bay, acidic enough to dissolve vessel hulls.

What We Did

The crew breached the reception and rooftop data nodes, gaining certain "privileges" within Omniagri's systems.

"A raging success. We got the dirt... literally (hummus) on the Corp."

NightCorp link discovered: Evidence of shady deals between NightCorp and Omniagri on emission levels.

See Purchase History for spending this session.

  • LCRA Investigation — Sum Ting: investigate unacceptable losses on LCRA goods being moved on the rerouted trade route (San Morro Bridge closure). 1000 eb + 1 free HQ upgrade. See Lifestyle and HQ.

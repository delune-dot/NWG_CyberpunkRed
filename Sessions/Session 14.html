Location: Omniagri Night City Regional Suites, Upper Marina
Status: 🔄 IN PROGRESS

Situation at Session Start

The Edgerunners are inside Omniagri's corporate warrens. Time is limited.

NCPD are en route — responding to the Imp alarm that Null Pointer scrubbed from the rooftop subnet, but not before it dispatched.

Current position: The crew has cracked reception and rooftop data nodes (Alt-me privileges granted within the building). Now pushing toward the managerial mainframe on Level 21.

The Breaking Development

The Screamsheet (nudged to the crew by their Alt-Mes) painted the external picture while we were inside:

A phalanx of NCPD cruisers arrived — but were immediately stalled by an Omniagri corporate suit feeding them corporate bylaws and "active-threat mitigation protocols."

While the NCPD were flipping through manuals, a convoy of Maelstrom rigs pulled up at the service entrance. Security opened the gates wide. Maelstrom deployed as Omniagri's private enforcement wing — surgical, armed, ready to clear the house.

"If you're inside that tower right now, you aren't waiting for the police. You're waiting for a slaughter."

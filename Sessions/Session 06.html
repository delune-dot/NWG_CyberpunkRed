Session 06 — Club Atlantis & Silk Du

← Session 05 · Session Log · Home · Session 07 →

Locations: Club Atlantis (The Glen), Jianmen Subway Station
Key Contacts: Sutton Lee, Sum Ting
Gigs: Trun Disintegrator return (Sutton Lee); Assassinate Silk Du (LCRA — 2000 eb each)

Club Atlantis — Meeting Sutton Lee

The crew had politely requested to meet the owner of the Trun Disintegrator they recovered from Chrome Cross. Their request was met with an invitation to Club Atlantis in The Glen.

"Situated in the lower of the two towers which make The Piper Memorial Sports Arena. It is an expensive establishment popular with movers, shakers, and power players. Decorated in a disorienting style reminiscent of M.C. Escher's House of Stairs..."

The crew checked in bulky armour and weapons at the upper cloakrooms. Scanned by "Nah Nah" staff before entering.

Vyper rapped. Null Pointer did interpretive dance. Everyone was moved to tears. MVP of the social session: Null Pointer.

Sutton Lee's Reception

Se greeted the crew coolly, inspecting a trinket with the focus of a jeweler. Those striking golden-brown eyes held cool assessment and faint annoyance.

"The item. It's back in its case. Intact. Y'all did good work, couriers."
"My people assured me that dealing with the retrieval agents directly was... unnecessary. Frankly, a waste of my finite time."
"To see so much potential in this crew of yours, wasted on such... pedestrian applications... is a shame. What does a group with your obvious talent want from me?"

See Sutton Lee for full profile and intel.

The Silk Du Gig

Sum Ting (sharply dressed as ever) briefed the crew on the next LCRA job.

The Weng Fang Tong crime syndicate — known in street slang as the "Ear Tong" for taking ears as trophies — had been flexing and disrupting the reputable business of Mr. Ling Po.

Target: Silk Du, the Weng Fang Tong's White Paper Fan (administrator). Stronghold: Jianmen Subway Station, disused, repurposed as their bunker beneath the ruins of the Hot Zone.

Sum Ting presented an ornamental ceremonial dagger — wavy, asymmetric blade like a Kris, pommel and guard in the style of a golden dragon. "Leave this embedded in the deceased."

Rate: 2000 eb each. This is dangerous territory.

Recon: Jianmen Subway Station

The subway station formerly handled 210,000+ commuters daily. Now heavily guarded by surveillance and static defense systems. Sum Ting suggested that desperate Scavs or Maelstrom Slave Crews in the ruins might offer insight into alternative access points.

Entering the Subway

The crew accessed the lower levels. Along the way they encountered a Maelstrom salvage crew. The overseer tried the usual bully approach — it didn't end well for him (now a bundle of parts stuffed in a vent). The crew had the foresight to put a tracking beacon on their hidden stash.

Taking out a salvage crew didn't go well with other roaming Maelstrom cyberpsychos. A game of cybercat & mouse followed — the crew trying to stay one step ahead of a borged-out death squad.

Silver lining: while hurriedly traversing connecting tunnels, the crew identified the heavily guarded escalator access to the Weng Fang Tong strongpoint.

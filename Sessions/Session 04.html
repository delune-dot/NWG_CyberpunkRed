Session 04 — Valkyrie Velocity / Just Like Donkey Kong

← Session 03 · Session Log · Home · Session 05 →

Locations: San Morro Bridge / NCART Train Level, Guangbo Tower (Neon & Nunchucks event)
Key Contacts: Darril, Jamal
Gig: Darril's extruder lift from the Red Chrome Legion

Valkyrie Velocity — The NCART Heist

Darril's gig: lift a pair of consumer-grade extruders (with provided serial numbers) from an RCL shipment.

Rather than push through the cramped Ribbon Town of the San Morro Bridge, the crew contracted Jamal — a local transport worker willing to brave inner-city streets — who sped them to a drop-off point in the middle of an 8-lane expressway. He demanded payment before they exited onto the busy thoroughfare.

The crew used the bridge's infrastructure to access the NCART train level, squeezing through tight conduit spaces to completely bypass the approach to the RCL's secure holdout. They dropped in on the lounging guards.

A brief combat followed. The RCL's poor quality weapons played a decisive role in their defeat. With the western portion of the repurposed NCART train secured, the crew barricaded the central compartment while they recovered the extruders.

While there: Uncovered RCL plans to disrupt an upcoming event in Little China — the same event as an upcoming gig for Goto Dengo.

On the way out: Left all barricaded doors open to the faceless of Ribbon Town as a screen. Also rigged a hastily-salvaged flamethrower trap.

Just Like Donkey Kong — Night Market

The crew identified a second night market this week, set up on the third level of a multilevel car park adjacent to South Cargo Village, under Pacific Blvd, canal side of the Skyline South merge. Territory on the cusp of South Night City, The Glen, and the Old Combat Zone.

Hosted by the Kanzaki Family. See Night Market.

The Neon & Nunchucks Event (Guangbo Tower)

Goto Dengo's gig: the event at Guangbo Tower took place 3 days after the RCL encounter, allowing the crew time to heal and prepare.

"Neon & Nunchucks: 70 Years of Hong Kong's Cinematic Chaos" — an immersive exhibition at Guangbo Tower.

The RCL (in line with the discovered plans) launched their assault on the tower during the event. Fire, panic, and chaos ensued. The fast-thinking crew spotted another crew on a Rodney mission and swept them along.

When things fell apart, the Edgerunners threw Rodney's crew under the bus to avoid Mr Ling Po's wrath. This act of betrayal — combined with the fact that no hospitality staff were harmed — saved their bacon. But Ling Po was still furious. Restitution demanded.

Sum Ting (LCRA representative) assigned to handle them.

Outcomes

  • ✅ Extruders recovered for Darril's gig
  • ⚠️ Threw Rodney's crew under the bus — now owe Sum Ting / LCRA restitution
  • 🔗 Sum Ting becomes the primary LCRA contact → leads to Session 05 Chrome Cross gig

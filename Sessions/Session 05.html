Session 05 — The Expendables

← Session 04 · Session Log · Home · Session 06 →

Locations: Chrome Cross, Little China
Key Contacts: Sum Ting, Darril
Gig: Chrome Cross distraction (for Gold Dragons, via LCRA restitution) — 1000 eb each

The Brief

Sum Ting, representing Mr Ling Po and the LCRA, assigned the crew a hard target as restitution for the Guangbo Tower incident. The Gold Dragons were planning a retaliatory move against the Red Chrome Legion — specifically, an assassination attempt against Vox, the RCL's Reichsführer.

The crew's job: be a distraction. Draw as many RCL to the Chrome Cross — their stronghold — as possible.

Chrome Cross: Situated in the most lawless part of Little China, still largely affected by 4th Corporate War destruction. A repurposed bar with a damaged structure teetering on collapse. Parking lot with salvaged cars, mismatched paint, and badly-spelled hate-speech graffiti. Full demolition would have high civilian collateral — the most desperate faceless live packed in tight around this Nazi bunker.

Sum Ting was vague about what the distraction should look like.

What Happened

The crew grapple-gunned onto the roof of Chrome Cross and descended through the structure.

Inside they found Vox, Popili, and the Brimstone Shotgun.

They killed Vox and Popili, recovered the shotgun and a mountain of loot.

The Fallout — The Commendables

Through unofficial channels: the crew's "distraction" was the precursor to the Gold Dragons' assassination attempt against Vox. The Edgerunners getting into the firefight and hostage situation with Popili sent the RCL into scramble mode. The Gold Dragons struck — but unsuccessfully.

Sum Ting relayed Mr. Ling Po's praise. Bonus awarded: a new home.

The LCRA offered a one-month reduced-rate rental on a Two-Bedroom Apartment (normally 2500 eb/month, offered at 2000 eb) at Prosperity Gardens Tenements, centrally located in North Little China, with 40 bonus HQIP.

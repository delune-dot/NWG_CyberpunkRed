Night Market

Home · Gig Board · Wishlist · Finances

Night Markets are irregular pop-up events offering Very Expensive (1000eb+) items unavailable at standard vendors. With a Fixer in the party, Expensive items (500eb) are accessible without trouble. Very Expensive items (1000eb+) require the fixer's contacts.

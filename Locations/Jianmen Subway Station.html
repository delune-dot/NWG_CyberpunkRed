Jianmen Subway Station (Former WFT Stronghold)

Little China · World Notes · Home

District: Beneath the Hot Zone / Little China border
Former Controller: Weng Fang Tong
Status: Cleared. WFT destroyed as a faction after Sessions 6–7.

Description

A disused subway station, repurposed by the Weng Fang Tong as their bunker and foothold in the area. At its peak as a transit hub, Jianmen handled over 210,000 commuters daily.

Beneath the ruins of the Hot Zone. The approach was heavily guarded by surveillance and static defense systems. Access was limited — the WFT had blocked most entry points. Sum Ting suggested that Scavs or Maelstrom Slave Crews in the ruins might know alternative access routes.

The surrounding Hot Zone had Maelstrom salvage crews operating, making the approach dangerous in multiple directions.

Layout (as encountered)

  • Multiple levels — upper approaches, lower platforms, connecting tunnels
  • Flooded elevator shaft — used by the crew as entry to the WFT level
  • WFT lounge — where Silk Du was found dealing with Eastern Tiger Triad
  • Escalator access — heavily guarded, identified in Session 6 reconnaissance
  • Connecting tunnels — used for Maelstrom evasion

Events Here

Session 6 (Part 1) — Reconnaissance

Crew accessed the lower levels. Encountered and killed a Maelstrom salvage crew (overseer turned to parts). Maelstrom cybercat-and-mouse chase followed through the tunnels. Identified the heavily guarded WFT escalator access. Placed a tracking beacon on hidden stash.

Session 7 — The Assassination

Entered via the flooded elevator shaft. Vyper forced open the elevator door. Inside: Silk Du trying to sell out his boss to Eastern Tiger Triad goons.

Fight in the lounge. Vyper killed Silk Du with the ceremonial Gold Dragon dagger. Pulse took the photo. Maelstrom finished the remaining WFT.

Silk Du's intel revealed the WFT cybernetics shipment route — the Level 3 sewage conduits under the Morro from Heywood Docks.

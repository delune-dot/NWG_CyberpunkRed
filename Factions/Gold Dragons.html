Gold Dragons

Factions and Intel · Home

Type: Gang (endorsed by LCRA)
Territory: Little China
Head: David Ling Ho
Disposition: Neutral / Ally of convenience

Overview

The Gold Dragons control Little China — and they are sanctioned and endorsed by the LCRA. This makes them unusual among Night City gangs: they function more like a civic police force than a typical boostergang, which is part of why Little China has one of the lowest nightly body counts in the combat zones.

They are fundamentally commerce-focused. Disorder is bad for business, and bad for the LCRA's legitimacy.

Key Figure

David Ling Ho — Head of the Gold Dragons. Owns Guangbo Tower. Hired the crew to recover the Brimstone Shotgun (Session 3–4).

Relationship With the Crew

Event Context
S3 — Brimstone Shotgun David Ling Ho caught the crew snooping at Guangbo Tower. Hired them to recover the RCL-stolen shotgun.
S4 — Chrome Cross Gold Dragons used our Chrome Cross distraction to attempt an assassination of Vox (RCL's Reichsführer). Attempt failed. We killed Vox ourselves.
LCRA alignment The crew's good standing with the LCRA translates to general tolerance from the Gold Dragons.

Stance on Red Chrome Legion

The Gold Dragons and RCL were in direct conflict throughout the early campaign. The Gold Dragons attempted (and failed) to assassinate Vox during our Chrome Cross distraction. The crew finished the job — effectively doing the Gold Dragons' work for them.

The RCL is destroyed. Little China is consolidated under Gold Dragon control.

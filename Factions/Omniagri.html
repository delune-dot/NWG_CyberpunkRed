Omniagri

Factions and Intel · Home

Type: Agri-corporation
Territory: Night City Regional Suites, Upper Marina
Disposition: Hostile — active target
Current Status: Under active infiltration (Sessions 12–14). NCPD + Maelstrom responding.

Overview

Omniagri is an aggressive agricultural corporation pursuing market monopoly in Night City's Synth Chicken market. Their strategy: destroy independent food producers through corporate viruses, nano-parasites, and hired muscle, until only Omniagri's Synth Chicken Franchelettes remain.

Key partner in crime: NightCorp. Evidence of shady deals on emission levels obtained by the crew in Session 12–13.

The Roost vs Omniagri

The Roost is a cooperative farmer group fighting for survival against Omniagri's "positional slippage." Our client Mamma Jamma is the Roost's contact for the Get Clucked! gig.

Mamma Rodriues (an independent farmer) was killed by Fallanx (a Maelstrom enforcer on Omniagri's payroll) — and the execution was posted on scream feeds as a warning to other holdouts.

The Kernel Colonels (KC), a Synth Chicken outlet previously associated with Mamma Jamma, has already been absorbed into Omniagri's asset list.

The Building — Night City Regional Suites

Located in Upper Marina, beneath the Del Cordando Overpass, adjacent to Ruggles Way. Brutalist concrete base transforming into a petrochemical refinery aesthetic at height.

Staff atmosphere: Uniformly sour and disgruntled — even the local AIs emulate the same attitude.

Bay access: Impossible. Omniagri's waste extruders discharge super-concentrated excrement that dissolves hull materials. Not even floathomes occupy that part of the bay.

The Maelstrom Angle

Omniagri uses Maelstrom as a private enforcement wing — openly and legally (via corporate jurisdiction clauses). When the NCPD arrived outside in response to our incursion, Omniagri's corporate suit stalled them with legal paperwork while Maelstrom walked through the service entrance.

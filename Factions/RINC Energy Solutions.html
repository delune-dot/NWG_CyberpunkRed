RINC Energy Solutions

Factions and Intel · Sutton Lee · Home

Type: Energy corporation
Key Figure: Sutton Lee (Executive Strategist, Special Projects)
Disposition: Neutral / Potential patron
Headquarters: RINC Exclusion Zone (former Bakersfield, ~200 miles from Night City)

Overview

RINC is a highly unusual corporation — it seeks to be forgotten while its influence grows. It operates outside direct conflict, preferring systematic economic pressure and strategic isolation over open warfare.

"They think they're rebuilding a destroyed kingdom, only this time with gigawatts instead of gunpowder."

History

Second Corporate War (2008–2010)

RINC emerged as a "shadow buyer" during the bloodiest years of the 2nd Corporate War between Petrochem and Sovoil. First major move: acquired the Kern River Oilfields from a cash-desperate Petrochem. Not a resource play — a strategic consolidation, demonstrating terrifying liquidity and timing.

Bakersfield — The Ghost Town Purchase

RINC negotiated directly with surviving fragments of the Department of the Interior, purchasing the entire jurisdiction of old Bakersfield under a "National Security Energy Development" clause. Overnight: RINC became the sole sovereign entity of the region.

The Isolation Protocol: Private security contractors tore up Route 99 entirely, redirecting all North/South traffic via I-5 to create a blind spot. The former city is now the RINC Exclusion Zone — heavily fortified, listed nowhere on Net maps.

Strategy — The Appomattox Protocol

"The fundamental strategic principle of the Lee model is to operate outside the theatre of direct conflict. Victory is achieved not through overwhelming force, but through the systematic demoralization and economic crippling of the opponent. The goal is always to compel an elegant, resource-saving concession."

RINC's asset vehicle: the Appomattox Trust (named for the Civil War surrender site). Motto rumoured to be "The Cause Endures." Security wear grey and gold uniforms.

Our Relationship With RINC

The crew recovered the Trun Disintegrator from the Chrome Cross RCL raid (Session 5). The item belonged to Sutton Lee. Returning it led to the Club Atlantis meeting and a potential ongoing relationship.

Current status: Sutton Lee is aware of the crew's competence. Se offered the question: "What does a group with your obvious talent want from me?" — not yet answered definitively.

The H-Town Handover gig may be RINC-adjacent (Club Atlantis as the drop point is suggestive).

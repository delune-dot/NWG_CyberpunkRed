Mamma Jamma — The Roost

NPCs · Omniagri · Home

Affiliation: The Roost (cooperative farmer group)
Role: Contact, client
Status: Active — ongoing gig client
Disposition: Positive — desperate and determined

Profile

Mamma Jamma is the crew's primary contact within The Roost — a cooperative farming operation scratching a living growing crops and raising animals outside of corporate patent viruses and nano-parasites.

She contacted Pinky directly for the Omniagri gig, having worked with a crew on The Roost's behalf before and trusting Pinky as a repeat contact.

The Roost

A cooperative farmer group defying corporate food monopoly. Omniagri is aggressively trying to destroy them through:
- Corporate viruses and nano-parasites targeting independent crops
- "Positional slippage" — buying out or forcing out independents
- Hired Maelstrom muscle (Fallanx) — who killed Mamma Rodriues (another farmer) and posted the execution as a warning

The Roost's goal: find evidence or leverage to make Omniagri stop. Not destruction — the corporate zone repercussions would be too severe.

The Gig

Get Clucked! — 1000 eb to infiltrate Omniagri and breach their servers for evidence/leverage.
- No structural damage (Upper Marina legal risk)
- Evidence of NightCorp / Omniagri emissions deals found (Session 12)
- Operation ongoing as of Session 14

Kernel Colonels / KC

Mamma Jamma was also previously associated with the Kernel Colonels / KC — an independent Synth Chicken outlet that has since been absorbed by Omniagri. Fallanx the Maelstrom enforcer "had his way" and it's now on Omniagri's asset list.

Assessment

A genuine underdog client. The cause is legitimate. The Roost is outgunned, outmonied, and outlawyered by Omniagri. The evidence we're after could be genuinely impactful — if we survive to deliver it.

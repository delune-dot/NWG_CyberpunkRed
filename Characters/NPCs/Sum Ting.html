Sum Ting — LCRA Representative

NPCs · LCRA · Home

Affiliation: LCRA (Little China Redevelopment Association)
Role: Jiyao Mishu (Personal Secretary / Hospitality Subordinate) of Mr Ling Po
Status: Active — our primary LCRA contact
Disposition: Neutral → Positive (earned through results)

Profile

Sum Ting is described as "sharply dressed as ever." He handles the LCRA's relationships with external operatives — including the Edgerunners — on behalf of Mr. Ling Po, who does not deal directly with crews like ours.

He is courteous and professional. Delivers difficult asks with politeness. His manner is measured; he doesn't threaten — he presents facts and expects competent follow-through.

How We Met

After the Guangbo Tower incident (Session 4) — where the crew threw Rodney's operatives under the bus during the chaos, and the RCL assault disrupted Ling Po's hospitality — Ling Po was furious. But because the crew hadn't harmed any hospitality staff, they weren't destroyed. Instead, they were assigned to Sum Ting for restitution gigs.

Notable Moments

  • Assigned the Chrome Cross distraction and praised the crew's "bravery" afterward
  • Presented the ceremonial Gold Dragon dagger for the Silk Du assassination — a very deliberate and theatrical object
  • Delivered Mr. Ling Po's praise and the bonus HQ offer after Chrome Cross
  • Offered the road investigation gig ("quietly informs you of the dastardly deeds")
  • Expressed quiet disappointment that San Morro Bridge closure was hurting LCRA commerce

Assessment

A professional handler. Not a street operator — he works through courtesy and leverage. The LCRA's problems are his problems to solve cleanly. He seems genuinely impressed by the crew's capability and has been escalating their pay accordingly.

Threat level: Low (while we remain useful).
Opportunity: Ongoing — the LCRA is a consistent, paying client with legitimate objectives.

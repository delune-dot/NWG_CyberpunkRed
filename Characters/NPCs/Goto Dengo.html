Goto Dengo (Deceased)

NPCs · Home · Session 09

Status: ☠️ Deceased — found dead in his apartment (Session 9), asphyxiated
Affiliation: Elflines / collector community
Known Relatives: Horse (nephew)

Profile

A corpulent Elflines Online collector and enthusiast, based in New Macau, Little China. Goto communicated primarily via his Elflines avatar — never in person if he could help it.

His collecting focus: rare Elflines items (the virtual collectibles from the Elflines Native America Online game). He hired the crew to assist with retrieving and trading in this niche market.

Relationship With the Crew

Goto Dengo was the party's first proper ongoing patron. He:
- Hired the crew for the Elflines collectibles retrieval (Session 3 area)
- Offered 1000 eb each for work around the Trun Disintegrator lead
- Was constantly messaging the crew via his Elflines avatar with new ideas
- Was found dead before paying out his owed contract

"Goto Dengo is blowing up your phones. Ever since he got word that you had retrieved the Trun Disintegrator he has not stopped messaging you (via his Elflines Avatar, as usual)."

Death

Found asphyxiated in his apartment (Session 9). The crew discovered the body after the Scarlett Lady disaster. Circumstances of his death remain unexplained in-session — likely killed during an interrogation by parties unknown.

The crew fleeced the place. Key finds:
- Spider Cyberchair with Heavyweight mods — now Antoine's
- Access to his Segotari Elflines account — where the Wyld West episode was hidden

The Legacy — Elflines Arc

Goto's Segotari account (via his AI agent Chief Heavy Runner) held:
1. Season 5, Episode 9 of Wyld West — the penultimate episode. Now in the party's possession.
2. A locked "paywall" — access requires the Sasquatch Fang from the Bitteroot Frost Caves

The crew completed the Frost Caves quest (Sessions 10–11) and unlocked Goto's reward.

Chief Heavy Runner

Goto's AI Agent. Still operates from within the lodge. Cooperative with Null Pointer's outside influence. Considers itself a loyal caretaker of Goto's digital estate.

Connection to Horse

Horse is Goto's nephew. He met the crew online through Elflines channels and joined after Goto's death. He has a personal stake in the Goto arc and the Elflines world.

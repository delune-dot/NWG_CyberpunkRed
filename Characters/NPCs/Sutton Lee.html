First Meeting — Club Atlantis (Session 6)

The club's ambient soundtrack vibrated the very air. Se was seated in a private corner, inspecting a small trinket with the intense focus of a jeweler. Completely undisturbed by the visual pandemonium.

"The item. It's back in its case. Intact. Y'all did good work, couriers." (The word 'couriers' delivered with subtle dismissive emphasis.)

"My people assured me that dealing with the retrieval agents directly was... unnecessary. Frankly, a waste of my finite time."

"To see so much potential in this crew of yours, wasted on such... pedestrian applications as tracking down lost trinkets, is a shame. Tell me, before I send you on your way, what is your agenda? What does a group with your obvious talent want from me?"

Corporate Profile (Surface Level)

Role: "Optimization of Strategic Acquisition and Risk Mitigation" for RINC's infrastructure ventures.

RINC Quarterly Quote: "RINC's mission is to secure the future of power. We believe in efficiency not merely as a cost-saving measure, but as a form of commanding authority."

Financial History

3 Years Ago: The Savannah Lineage Fund — known for holding vast tracts of pre-Collapse North American land and historical artifacts — liquidated its core assets. Orchestrated by Sutton Lee. Injected billions into energy and infrastructure sectors.

"Critics suggest the move is less about diversification and more about leveraging old-world debt to purchase new-world power. The fund's motto, long rumoured to be 'The Cause Endures,' has become a point of controversy."

Intelligence (Deep Web)

Encrypted file fragment — R.E.L. Logic Branch:

"...the fundamental strategic principle of the Lee model is to operate outside the theatre of direct conflict. Victory is achieved not through overwhelming force, but through the systematic demoralization and economic crippling of the opponent. The goal is always to compel an elegant, resource-saving concession. This is the 'Appomattox Protocol' in action."

Hacker forum — u/Reconstruction_Era:

"That RINC executive is a ghost. Every algorithm, every asset, every line of code they touch is perfectly optimized for maximum resource extraction. It's not about money; it's about re-establishing a forgotten hierarchy. They think they're rebuilding a destroyed kingdom, only this time, they're using gigawatts instead of gunpowder. Avoid direct engagement — Sutton Lee sees everything as a chess game where they can sacrifice a pawn (you) to protect the Queen (RINC)."

The Appomattox Connection

  • The Appomattox Court House is the historical site where General Robert E. Lee surrendered, ending the American Civil War.
  • The asset vehicle is called the Appomattox Trust.
  • RINC motto: "The Cause Endures."
  • This person is deliberately invoking Confederate/Lost Cause symbolism through corporate and financial structures.

Assessment

Intelligent, patient, plays long games. Does not engage in direct conflict. Sees the crew as talented but currently wasted on small work. The question "What does a group with your obvious talent want from me?" is either a genuine offer or a test. Possibly both.

Threat level: High — if we become a liability.
Opportunity level: High — if we can be useful to RINC.
